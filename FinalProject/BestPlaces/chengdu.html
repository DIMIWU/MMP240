10 Best Places To Go

ChengDu

Chengdu, the capital of China's Sichuan province, is a central city in Western China. This integrated transport hub also serves as the technological, commercial and financial center of Southwest China. Thanks to a convenient transportation network, it is also a key hi-tech industrial base and a center for commerce and logistics.

Most tourist attractions in Chengdu are found in Qingyang District and Wuhou District: Du Fu's Thatched Cottage, Qingyang Temple, Wenshu Monastery, Kuan Zhai Alley and the Jinsha Site are all in Qingyang District, and Zhuge Liang Memorial Hall and Jinli Ancient Street are in Wuhou District.
