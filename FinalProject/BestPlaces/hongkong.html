Hong Kong

Beijing is the capital of China. As one of the world's largest cities, it enjoys significant international influence.

Beijing is one of "The Four Great Ancient Capitals of China". The city has over 3,000 years of construction history and has been the capital for 860 years. In the city, there are countless scenic spots and historical sites, featuring historic figures and cultural heritage. The city boasts historical sites such as the Forbidden City, one of the Five Most Beautiful Royal Palaces in the World; the Temple of Heaven, where the Emperors worship the Heaven, Beihai Park and the Summer Palace, which were imperial retreats, as well as sections of the Great Wall at Badaling, Mutianyu, and Simatai, and Prince Gong Mansion, the world's largest Siheyuan (courtyard house).
